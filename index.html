Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of inputs does TuNiaX accept?

Provide clinical data such as symptoms, pulmonary chest X-ray findings, or a combination of both. Use concise sentences and separate key findings with commas for optimal results.

How are search results ranked?

TuNiaX prioritizes the most relevant results at the top. To ensure thoroughness, we recommend exploring all results provided.

Tip: Some search results have images that can help you quickly judge the match of a disease.

Can I refine my search?

Absolutely! Modify and combine symptoms, X-ray findings, or other clinical inputs to refine your focus on dominant disease characteristics.

What can I do with the results?

TuNiaX aids healthcare professionals in hypothesis generation and decision-making. When available, associated genes and approved treatments for tuberculosis and pneumonia are listed alongside results.

Why no consent box?

TuNiaX employs cookieless tracking to prioritize privacy. Our system refreshes clientIDs every five days, ensuring compliance with privacy standards and disabling long-term tracking.
